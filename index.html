How to use

1. Find the department offering the course you want to study (e.g., Chemistry for CHM203).
2. Click the department card to open it.
3. Choose your level (100, 200, 300, 400).
4. Browse courses and click a course to open its materials in Google Drive.
5. If you don't see a course here, check other departments — courses are sometimes shared.
