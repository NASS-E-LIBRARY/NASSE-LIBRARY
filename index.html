How to use

1. Look for the department offering the course you want to study (e.g., Chemistry for CHM203).
2. Click a department card to open it (only one stays open).
3. Choose your level (100, 200, 300, 400).
4. Browse courses and click a course to open materials in Google Drive.
5. If the course isn’t listed, check other departments — some are shared.
